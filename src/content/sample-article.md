## Introduction

Cloud costs can spiral out of control quickly. In this guide, we'll walk through proven techniques to optimize your AWS spend.

## Key Areas to Review

1. **Unused Resources** - EC2 instances, RDS databases, and load balancers that no one is using
2. **Right-sizing** - Moving from over-provisioned instances to the right size
3. **Reserved Instances** - Committing to usage patterns for discounts

## Conclusion

Start with these basics and you'll see immediate savings.
